# Magnificent 7 Financial Analysis Report
**Analysis Period:** FY 2023 - FY 2025 (Trailing 2 Years)
**Report Date:** February 2026

---

## Executive Summary

The Magnificent 7 (Apple, Microsoft, Alphabet, Amazon, Meta, Nvidia, Tesla) collectively represent the dominant force in global technology markets. Over the past two years, these companies have shown divergent trajectories:

| Company | FY2024 Revenue | FY2025 Revenue | YoY Growth | FY2025 Net Income | Profit Margin |
|---------|----------------|----------------|------------|-------------------|---------------|
| Apple | $391.0B | $416.2B | +6.4% | $112.0B | 26.9% |
| Microsoft | $245.1B | $281.7B | +14.9% | $101.8B | 36.2% |
| Alphabet | $350.0B | $402.8B | +15.1% | $132.2B | 32.8% |
| Amazon | $638.0B | $717.0B | +12.4% | $77.7B | 10.8% |
| Meta | $164.5B | $201.0B | +22.2% | $60.5B | 30.1% |
| Nvidia | $60.9B | $130.5B | +114.2% | $72.9B | 55.9% |
| Tesla | $97.7B | $94.8B | -2.9% | $3.8B | 4.0% |

**Key Themes:**
- **AI Dominance:** Nvidia's explosive growth (+114% YoY) driven by data center AI demand
- **Cloud Resilience:** Azure, AWS, and Google Cloud continue strong double-digit growth
- **Services Expansion:** Apple and Meta shifting toward higher-margin recurring revenue
- **Tesla Challenges:** Automotive revenue declining amid EV competition and margin pressure
- **Regulatory Risk:** Antitrust scrutiny intensifying across all Mag 7 companies
